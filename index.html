How Club Penguin Ruined My Winter Break

When I was little, my family had a laptop that was older than me.

One day, I discovered a website called Club Penguin.

The laptop would get really hot when playing that game, but i kept going on it anyways.

During a winter break I had a friend over and I really wanted her to play Club Penguin with me.

While we were playing, the laptop suddenly froze!

No matter what we tried, the screen wasn't moving.

My mom was angry at first, but decided that we needed a new computer anyways.

But, I decided never to play Club Penguin again...
